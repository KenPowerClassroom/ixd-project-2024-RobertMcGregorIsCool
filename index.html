Introduction

Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game contexts, such as education. The objective is to increase user motification, engagement and enjoyment.

This project involved various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping and usability testing. The competitive analysis will help me to identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, and highlight areas we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring that the user's needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimately improving the user's learning outcomes.
